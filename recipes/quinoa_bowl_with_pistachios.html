Strawberry-Banana Quinoa Bowl with Pistachios

Description

Here's a delightful snack idea for your ovulatory phase:

Ingredients

  • Cooked quinoa
  • Strawberries, diced
  • Banana, sliced
  • Pistachios, chopped
  • A sprinkle of cinnamon

Steps

  1. Prepare Quinoa: Cook the quinoa as per the package instructions and let it cool down a bit.
  2. Assemble the Bowl: In a bowl, mix a serving of the cooked quinoa with sliced bananas and strawberries.
  3. Add Toppings: Sprinkle chopped pistachios over the top for a nice crunch.
  4. Season: Lightly dust the bowl with cinnamon for a warm, slightly sweet flavor.
  5. Serve: Enjoy this bowl as a fresh, nutritious snack!

This bowl combines the wholesome goodness of quinoa with the freshness of strawberries and bananas, and the pistachios add a lovely crunch along with essential nutrients suitable for your ovulatory phase. The cinnamon adds a sweet aroma without extra sugar, making it a perfect snack that's sweet but not overly so. Enjoy!
